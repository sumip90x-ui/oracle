Item 5.07.



Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

(a) Coherent Corp. (the “Company”) held its Annual Meeting of Shareholders on November 13, 2025 (the “Annual Meeting”). As of September 15, 2025, the record date for the Annual Meeting (the “Record Date”), there were 156,935,310 shares of the Company’s common stock, no par value (“Common Stock”), outstanding and entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting. In addition, 75,000 shares of the Company’s Series
B-1
 Convertible Preferred Stock, no par value, and 140,000 shares of the Company’s Series
B-2
 Convertible Preferred Stock, no par value, were issued and outstanding as of the Record Date and entitled to vote as one class with the Common Stock on an
as-converted
 basis. A total of 163,694,370 votes outstanding, representing approximately 87.62% of the total number of votes entitled to be cast at the Annual Meeting, were present in person or by proxy at the Annual Meeting.
