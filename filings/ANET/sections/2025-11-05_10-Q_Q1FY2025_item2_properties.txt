Item 2
. Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities and Use of Proceeds
(c) Issuer Purchase of Equity Securities
From time to time, we repurchase shares of our common stock pursuant to repurchase programs that are funded from working capital. The Prior Repurchase Program, which allowed for stock repurchases of up to $1.2 billion, was completed in May 2025, and the New Repurchase Program allows for repurchases of an additional $1.5 billion. The New Repurchase Program does not obligate us to acquire any of our common stock and may be suspended or discontinued by the Company at any time without prior notice. We did not repurchase any shares during the three months ended September 30, 2025. During the nine months ended September 30, 2025, we repurchased a total of $921.0 million of our common stock under our Prior Repurchase Program and $62.0 million of our common stock under our New Repurchase Program. As of September 30, 2025, the remaining authorized amount for repurchases under the New Repurchase Program was $1.4 billion. Refer to Note 6.
