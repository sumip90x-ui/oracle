Item 2
. Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities and Use of Proceeds
(c) Issuer Purchase of Equity Securities
From time to time, we repurchase shares of our common stock pursuant to repurchase programs that are funded from working capital. In May 2025, our board of directors authorized the $1.5 billion Repurchase Program. The Repurchase Program does not obligate us to acquire any of our common stock and may be suspended or discontinued by the Company at any time without prior notice. We did not repurchase any shares during the three months ended March 31, 2026. As of March 31, 2026, the remaining authorized amount for repurchases under the Repurchase Program was $817.9 million.
